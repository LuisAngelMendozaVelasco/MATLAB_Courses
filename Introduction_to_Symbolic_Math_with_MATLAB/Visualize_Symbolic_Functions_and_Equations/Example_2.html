Task 2

By default, fsurf plots over the interval [-5 5] for both x and y. You can plot over a different interval by specifying the interval in the fsurf function.
fsurf(h,[lowerbound upperbound])
fsurf(f, [0 2])

Task 3

Surfaces can also be defined by equations. The fimplicit3 function plots the surface defined by an equation of three variables.
fimplicit3(equation)
