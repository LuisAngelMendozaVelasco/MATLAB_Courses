Task 1

The nth root of a number a can be found by solving the equation
x^n−a=0
Consider 3√8. In this example, n=3 and a=8.
fun38 = @(x) x.^3 - 8
fun38 = function_handle with value:
@(x)x.^3-8

Task 4
