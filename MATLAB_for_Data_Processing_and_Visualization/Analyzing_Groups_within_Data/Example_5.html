Calculating the maximum windspeed over land and over sea for each hurricane may be easier to analyze if the table were structured with one row for each hurricane and then a column for the land and sea measurements.
When calculating other group summary statistics, you automatically get the GroupCount variable, which you may not want after unstacking the data.
overLandAndSeaWS = removevars(overLandAndSeaWS, "GroupCount")
