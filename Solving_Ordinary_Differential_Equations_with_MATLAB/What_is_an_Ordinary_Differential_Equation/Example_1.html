This code loads and plots the altitude data.

t = shuttledata.t;

grid on
title("Shuttle Altitude")
xlabel("time (s)")
ylabel("altitude (m)")

You can calculate the shuttle's velocity by finding the total change in height, Δh, and dividing by the total change in time, Δt.
v=Δh/Δt
